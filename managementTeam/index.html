Mr. Priyam Khare

Head Of Operations

Mr. Khare has experience in the financial industry with a career spanning more than 7years, in Microfinance credit (MFI) and MSME lending. He has been instrumental in more than 7 states during his association with different MFI companies. He is experienced in handling verticals of field and systems operations. A post graduate in rural management from IIFM Bhopal and BBA in finance. Earlier Mr. Khare was associated with Basix Sub k I Transaction Ltd and Sahayog Microfinance Ltd. He has a keen interest in reading and is a cricket enthusiast.
